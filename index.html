About Me

I am a Grade 10 student from India with a deep commitment to mathematics, research, and engineering. My work is grounded in first principles—whether in abstract mathematical theory or in applied systems—and driven by a long-term aspiration to become an aerospace engineer, working at the intersection of advanced mathematics, physics, and real-world engineering challenges.

My journey began with competitive mathematics and has evolved into original research in numerical analysis, interpolation, and graph theory. I aim to pursue aerospace engineering with a strong mathematical foundation, contributing original ideas that connect theory, computation, and engineering design.
